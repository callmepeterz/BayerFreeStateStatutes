**Amendment the First**

__Section I__: "and matters in Congress" in Article I Section 6 is hereby omitted.

__Section II__: Article II Section 2 is hereby replaced with the following clause:
    "Every citizen upon being appointed by one of the members of the contemporary Congress and confirmed by at least 2/3 of Congress without any veto from any member shall be granted a seat in Congress with all of its granted rights, duties, and privileges as prescribed by this Constitution, and shall hold this seat at the discretion of Congress."
